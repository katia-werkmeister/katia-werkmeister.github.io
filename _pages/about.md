I am a second-year PhD student at the [ifo Center for the Economics of Education](https://www.ifo.de/en/research/ifo-center-for-the-economics-of-education) and the [Faculty of Economics at LMU Munich](https://www.econ.lmu.de/en/).

I am an applied microeconomist, with a focus on labor economics and the economics of education. I am particularly interested in (in-)formal institutions and the education-to-work transition. My work combines large-scale data with standard datasets. 

I hold a M.Sc. in Economic and Social Sciences from Bocconi University in Milan, a B.Sc. in Economics from LMU Munich, and a B.A. in Political Science from LMU Munich.
