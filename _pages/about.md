I am a PhD student at the [ifo Center for the Economics of Education](https://www.ifo.de/en/research/ifo-center-for-the-economics-of-education) and the [Faculty of Economics at LMU Munich](https://www.econ.lmu.de/en/).

I am an applied microeconomist, with a focus on labor economics and the economics of education. I am particularly interested in (in-)formal institutions and novel data sources.
